Artist Statement

To create art work, it is required to be creative, since you need to make your art work stand out among many pieces. When I draw more and more pieces of drawing, it will help me build my skills and also be easy for me to come up with more mature creative ideas, and to show them effectively. Finally, it is a really great and direct way to record the beauty in the life. Art work is really vivid for people to see how real views are and how actually beautiful they are. For me, an image is more memorable than a piece of article, and it is my another motive of doing art. Overall, chasing new skills, making myself become more creative and recoding beauties in our life in a vivid way are the motive of doing art for me.

Description:

Lonely

Description:

This is drawn by chalks, and the idea is from a poem that was chosen in the class. There is a man standing on a ship that is travelling in the lake at night, on the ship, you have a good view of Toronto. It is also lonely for him to stand there alone. It is also a reflection of me who come to this remote country alone without any friends, family members and support here, it is such a lonely time.

Trance

Description:

This is a bust of a partner’s head, it contains the facial features of head, hair, eyes, nose, ears, mouth and neck, it is used clay and lots of paper and masking tape to finally finish it. It is named Trance showing a person is droopy, it is a state of many teenagers nowadays, they are not as active as they should be at this age.

Life

Description:

This is a drawing of still life, it has a yellow pen, a pencil sharpener, a ink bottle and a candy case. They are all put on a desk. It also has a purple background. Pen represents my study, and other objects represents what and who help me for this years, such as pencil sharpener represents teacher who correct my mistakes all the time.
